About Me

Business Owner at Mercedes Santana Design

Mercedes Santana is a fashion designer with more than 30 years of experience. She has always worked on wedding, gala and celebrity dresses, hers. She has worked alongside Dominican designers; Mercy Jacquez, Jorge Diez and Giannina Azzar where she was also an educator at the Mercy Jacquez Higher Institute. Her atelier Merfasf_ MercedesSantanaDesigner had its beginnings in the Dominican Republic, continuing in the United States.

In her career, she has stood out as a Haute Couture Designer and it fits perfectly with her clients, thus making a difference in the way she dresses... her frequent phrase is "A well-dressed woman, she never goes unnoticed.
